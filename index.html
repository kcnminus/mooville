About Us

MooVille Cookies & Cream is owned and run by a mother and daughter team. Robin previously owned an ice cream parlor and truck in Wyoming. Her daughter, Emily, is a graduate of the Culinary Institute of America, with a baking and pastry degree and ten years of experience in the industry as a baker and a manager. After moving to middle Tennessee, they decided to work together and came up with the idea of combining delicious, made-from-scratch cookies with various flavors of premium Blue Bell ice cream. MooVille Cookies & Cream's menu consists of seasonal ice cream sandwiches and year-long mainstays, such as warm cookie sundaes and classic bowls and scoops.
